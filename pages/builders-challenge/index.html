The challenge

We’re at a tipping point. It’s never been more essential that we create a movement toward trustworthy AI — and this requires collaboration with the brightest minds working on AI technology today.

Join us for the Mozilla Builders: Responsible AI Challenge, and be part of creating a future where AI benefits humanity.

The opportunity

In addition to a $50k in prizes, this contest offers a unique chance to showcase your talents and receive mentorship from some of the world’s leading thinkers, technologists, ethicists and business people who can help guide you with your own responsible AI project.

Enter your email address to get updates about the Responsible AI Contest including participating mentors, judges, prizes and more.

Applications open starting March 30, 2023.
