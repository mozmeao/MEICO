Your opportunity

Network with other innovators

Connect with a community of like-minded innovators who want to create healthier internet experiences and build relationships that can help support your journey.

Pitch your idea and access mentorship

Attend workshops and listen to speakers discussing topics such as algorithmic bias, evaluation and transparency. Additionally, take the opportunity to pitch your idea live.

Unlock resources

With our partners providing various technology resources, we'll supply you with the proper support to bring your idea to life. Plus, tap into our 50k prize money pool.

Things you should know

The challenge is open to everyone regardless of geographical location* but you must be present for the in-person event.

The requirements

  • Project must be no more than 90 days old by March 30th, 2023
  • Make sure you have have rights to any IP, code or data in use
  • Individuals and teams can apply
  • No cap on number of teammates

The key dates

  • March 30th: Applications open
  • April 20th: Applications close
  • May 8th: Top 10 finalists announced
  • May 31st: In-person event for top 10 finalists

The prizes

  • 1st Prize: $25,000
  • 2nd Prize: $15,000
  • 3rd Prize: $10,000

Modest travel and accommodation stipends will be provided for finalists. One winner for each prize.
